THE 4 PILLARS

The foundation of every effective AI prompt

Role

"Act as a senior sales consultant..."

Context

Background information
"The client is in automotive..."

Task

What exactly to do?
"Write a follow-up email..."

Format

How should output look?
"Keep it under 200 words..."

For the Pros
This framework is based on the KLAR/CRISPE model used in professional prompt engineering. Google's Whitepaper on Prompt Engineering (2024) confirms that role-based prompting improves output quality by up to 40%.
Google Prompting Whitepaper
Comparison

SPOT THE DIFFERENCE

Bad Prompt
Write an email to a customer.
Good Prompt
[ROLE] You are a professional sales representative at an automotive supply company. [CONTEXT] Customer ABC Motors in Munich requested a follow-up after our brake system meeting yesterday. [TASK] Write a follow-up email that thanks them, summarizes 3 key discussion points, and proposes a demo next week. [FORMAT] Keep under 200 words, professional but warm tone, include clear call-to-action.
